As of April 16, Windward Analytics confirmed 823 vessels remain stranded in the Persian Gulf or anchored off Oman. US naval enforcement is active, requiring cargo nationality declarations. Xeneta warns the ceasefire "is not enough to restore normal commercial operations." Insurance war-risk premiums remain at elevated levels.
尽管4月8日宣布美伊两周停火，霍尔木兹海峡航运仍处于"受控通行"状态，而非自由商业通航。截至4月16日，Windward Analytics确认仍有823艘船舶滞留波斯湾内或在阿曼湾外锚泊。美国海军执法仍处于活跃状态，要求申报货物国籍。Xeneta警告称停火"不足以恢复正常商业运营"。保险战争风险附加费仍维持高位。

Monitor Windward vessel tracker weekly. 霍尔木兹完全重开最少还需4-6周。CUL中东航线（杰贝阿里、达曼）应继续维持好望角绕行路由和现行附加费。估计额外成本：每趟中东航次$30-50万。与法务协调Q2合同中的战争风险条款。每周监控Windward船舶追踪器。

⭐ Drewry WCI Snaps Six-Week Rally — Falls 3% to $2,246/FEU as Tariff Surge Fades

⭐ Featured ⚡ CUL Direct Commercial Trade Network Global
The Drewry World Container Index (WCI) fell 3% this week (Week 16) to $2,246/FEU — ending a six-week rally driven by Middle East bunker volatility. All Asia-outbound lanes declined: Shanghai–Rotterdam -3% to $2,229; Shanghai–LA -3% to $2,810; Shanghai–New York -3% to $3,552; Shanghai–Genoa -2% to $3,343. The rally peaked after the US tariff deadline (Apr 9) cargo front-loading subsided. Meanwhile, Week 16 saw 43 blank sailings announced for Wks 13–17, with TPEB (transpacific eastbound) accounting for 58% of capacity cuts.
Drewry世界集装箱运价指数（WCI）本周（第16周）下跌3%至$2,246/FEU，终止了由中东燃油波动驱动的六周连涨。亚洲出口各航线全线下跌：上海—鹿特丹-3%至$2,229；上海—洛杉矶-3%至$2,810；上海—纽约-3%至$3,552；上海—热那亚-2%至$3,343。涨势随美国关税截止日（4月9日）前的抢运潮消退而见顶。第13-17周共宣布43个停航班次，跨太平洋东行占运力削减的58%。
🎯 CUL Impact: Rate correction confirms tariff front-loading exhaustion. CUL commercial teams should reassess Q2 spot rate positions — FAK and GRI momentum has stalled.
