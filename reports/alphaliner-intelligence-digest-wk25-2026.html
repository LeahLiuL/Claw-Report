⭐ Iran-US Peace MoU Signed June 19 — Hormuz Strait to Reopen
⭐ 伊美和平谅解备忘录6月19日签署——霍尔木兹海峡即将重开
⭐ Featured ⚡ CUL Direct Ops Network Legal Commercial Trade Middle East / Global
The Iran-US Memorandum of Understanding (Islamabad MoU) was signed on June 19 in Switzerland, following Iran's June 11 declaration of a total Hormuz blockade. The US naval blockade ended June 15, and the Strait is expected to reopen for minesweeping operations after the signing. This marks the most significant geopolitical shift for Gulf shipping since the February 27 strikes. Oil prices reacted: Brent initially spiked to $93+ on June 11 but has since moderated on peace expectations. Transit volumes had dropped to historic lows of 2–6 vessels/day vs. pre-crisis 95–138/day.
伊朗-美国谅解备忘录（伊斯兰堡备忘录）于6月19日在瑞士签署，此前伊朗于6月11日宣布全面封锁霍尔木兹海峡。美国海上封锁于6月15日结束，海峡预计在签署后重新开放以进行扫雷行动。这是自2月27日袭击以来海湾航运最重要的地缘政治转折。油价反应：布伦特6月11日一度飙升至$93+，后因和平预期回落。通行量已降至历史低点2-6艘/天，危机前为95-138艘/天。
🎯 CUL Impact: CRITICAL. Hormuz reopening is the single most important event for CUL's Middle East operations. However, full normalization will take months: minesweeping estimated 4–6 months, war risk premiums need 2–3 months to normalize, and major carriers (Maersk/MSC/CMA CGM) will observe conditions before resuming normal Gulf bookings. CUL should maintain Cape of Good Hope routing for July sailings and gradually transition from August. Priority: coordinate with slot partners (OOCL/Evergreen/Hapag-Lloyd) on Gulf service restoration timeline. Current Cape detour costs CUL est. $300–500K extra fuel per Middle East voyage — potential annual savings of $11–24M upon full normalization. 关键影响。霍尔木兹重开是CUL中东运营的最重要事件。但全面正常化需数月：扫雷预计4-6个月，战争险费率需2-3个月稳定，主要船公司（马士基/MSC/CMA CGM）将观察情况后才恢复常态订舱。CUL应维持7月航次绕行好望角，8月起逐步过渡。优先事项：与舱位合作伙伴（东方海外/长荣/赫伯罗特）协调海湾服务恢复时间表。当前绕行好望角每中东航次额外燃油$30-50万——全面正常化后年潜在节省$1100-2400万。

⭐ Container Freight Rates Surge to 18-Month High — Drewry WCI Hits $3,969/FEU
⭐ 集装箱运价飙升至18个月新高——Drewry WCI达$3,969/FEU
⭐ Featured ⚡ CUL Direct Commercial Trade Finance Global
Drewry's World Container Index surged 12% WoW to $3,969/FEU on June 18 — the highest level in 18 months. All major lanes rose: Shanghai–Rotterdam +15% to $4,342, Shanghai–Genoa +12% to $5,756, Shanghai–Los Angeles +10% to $5,142, Shanghai–New York +15% to $6,769. Drivers: early peak season demand, front-loading ahead of July 1 bunker adjustment factor (BAF) changes, and anticipated US tariff modifications. Carriers are successfully implementing FAK rate increases and PSS surcharges. MSC's emergency fuel surcharge (EFS) Asia–USEC rose to $644/FEU.
Drewry世界集装箱指数6月18日周涨12%至$3,969/FEU——18个月来最高水平。主要航线全线上涨：上海-鹿特丹+15%至$4,342，上海-热那亚+12%至$5,756，上海-洛杉矶+10%至$5,142，上海-纽约+15%至$6,769。驱动因素：旺季需求提前启动、7月1日燃油附加费（BAF）调整前抢运、美国关税预期变化。船公司成功实施FAK费率上调和PSS附加费。MSC亚洲-美东紧急燃油附加费（EFS）升至$644/FEU。
🎯 CUL Impact: Strong rate environment benefits CUL's revenue outlook. Asia–Europe and Asia–Med lanes (CUL core markets) are leading the surge. Recommend Commercial team to: (1) push through July GRI/FAK increases aligned with market, (2) secure medium-term contract positions while spot rates are high, (3) evaluate PSS application on Middle East/Red Sea lanes given Hormuz reopening premium. However, be cautious — Drewry expects further increases near-term but the market may correct once front-loading subsides. 强劲运价环境利好CUL收入前景。亚欧和亚地航线（CUL核心市场）领涨。建议商务团队：（1）顺势推动7月GRI/FAK上调，（2）趁即期运价高位锁定中期合同头寸，（3）评估中东/红海航线的PSS附加费（霍尔木兹重开溢价）。但需谨慎——Drewry预计短期内运价将继续上涨，但前置装载消退后市场可能回调。

Bunker Fuel: Rotterdam VLSFO at Global Low $612/mt — Fujairah Remains Elevated at $1,145/mt
船用燃油：鹿特丹VLSFO全球最低$612/吨——富查伊拉维持高位$1,145/吨
⚡ CUL Direct Ops Finance Global
As of June 19, VLSFO prices show stark regional divergence reflecting Hormuz disruption: Rotterdam $612/mt (global low, +$24 WoW), Singapore $670/mt (+$11), Houston $614/mt (-$18), Fujairah $1,145/mt (-$34 but still 87% above Rotterdam). The Fujairah premium reflects war risk and supply chain disruption in the Gulf. With the Iran-US MoU signed, Fujairah prices are expected to normalize gradually over 2–3 months. Global VLSFO 20-port average: $736/mt. Brent crude: ~$90/bbl range post-MoU announcement.
截至6月19日，VLSFO价格呈现明显区域分化，反映霍尔木兹中断影响：鹿特丹$612/吨（全球最低，周涨$24），新加坡$670/吨（+$11），休斯顿$614/吨（-$18），富查伊拉$1,145/吨（-$34但仍高出鹿特丹87%）。富查伊拉溢价反映海湾战争风险和供应链中断。随着伊美备忘录签署，富查伊拉价格预计将在2-3个月内逐步正常化。全球20港VLSFO均价：$736/吨。布伦特原油：备忘录公布后约$90/桶区间。
🎯 CUL Impact: Rotterdam ($612/mt) remains the optimal bunkering point — maximize fuel loading on Europe-bound and Europe-departing vessels. Singapore ($670) is reasonably priced. Fujairah ($1,145) should be avoided until war risk premiums subside (target: below $800). With Hormuz reopening on the horizon, plan for Fujairah/Salalah bunkering to resume from Q4 2026. EU ETS carbon cost: ~$292/mt additional on VLSFO (EUA €80/t). 鹿特丹（$612/吨）仍是最优加油点——欧洲方向和出发船舶应最大化加油量。新加坡（$670）价格合理。富查伊拉（$1,145）应避免，直至战争险溢价回落（目标：$800以下）。霍尔木兹即将重开，规划2026年Q4起恢复富查伊拉/萨拉莱加油。EU ETS碳成本：VLSFO额外约$292/吨（EUA €80/吨）。

Container Charter Market Remains Firm — New ConTex Rises to 1,554 (+12 pts)
集装箱租船市场持续坚挺——New ConTex升至1,554（+12点）
⚡ CUL Direct Equipment Finance Global
The New ConTex container charter index rose 12 points (+0.8%) to 1,554 in Week 25, continuing its firm trajectory. The charter market remains stable across all segments with no segment moving more than ±1%. Key drivers: Hormuz disruption keeping tonnage tied up on longer Cape routes, early peak season demand absorbing available capacity, and limited newbuilding deliveries in feeder sizes. 3,000+ TEU spot vessels remain virtually unavailable — all absorbed by carriers locking in tonnage during the Gulf crisis.
New ConTex集装箱租船指数第25周上涨12点（+0.8%）至1,554，延续坚挺走势。租船市场各船型段保持稳定，无船型段波动超过±1%。主要驱动因素：霍尔木兹中断使运力被锁定在更长好望角航线、旺季需求提前启动吸收可用运力、支线船型新船交付有限。3,000+ TEU现货船几乎无可用——全部被船公司在海湾危机期间锁定。
🎯 CUL Impact: Tight charter market continues to validate CUL's WK13 decision to lock in 4,810 TEU newbuild at $45–46K/day. Current spot rates for comparable tonnage would likely be higher. As Hormuz reopens, expect some charter rate softening on larger vessels but feeder sizes (1,700–2,500 TEU) will remain tight due to regional trade demand. CUL should monitor opportunities to release chartered tonnage if own-fleet capacity becomes sufficient post-Hormuz normalization. 租船市场紧张持续验证CUL第13周以$45-46K/天锁定4,810 TEU新造船的决策。当前同等吨位即期费率可能更高。随着霍尔木兹重开，预计大型船舶租船费率有所回落，但支线船型（1,700-2,500 TEU）因区域贸易需求将保持紧张。CUL应监控释放租赁吨位的机会（若自有运力在霍尔木兹正常化后变得充足）。

US Tariff Uncertainty Drives Transpacific Front-Loading — Bookings Surge
美国关税不确定性驱动跨太平洋抢运——订舱量激增
⚡ CUL Direct Commercial Trade Network Asia / Americas
Anticipated US tariff changes in July are driving a significant front-loading wave on transpacific lanes. Drewry reports Shanghai–LA rates up 10% WoW to $5,142/FEU, Shanghai–NY up 15% to $6,769/FEU. Carriers have announced 6 blank sailings for the coming week to manage capacity. The 90-day US-China tariff truce (announced April 22) approaches its mid-July expiry, creating urgency for importers to ship before potential reinstatement. MSC, CMA CGM and ONE have all announced July PSS/FAK increases.
预期美国7月关税变化正在推动跨太平洋航线大规模抢运潮。Drewry报告上海-洛杉矶周涨10%至$5,142/FEU，上海-纽约周涨15%至$6,769/FEU。船公司已宣布下周6个空白航班以管理运力。4月22日宣布的90天中美关税休战接近7月中旬到期，进口商急于在潜在恢复关税前出货。MSC、CMA CGM和ONE均已宣布7月PSS/FAK上调。
🎯 CUL Impact: CUL does not operate direct transpacific services, but the tariff-driven surge impacts Asia feeder networks: increased intra-Asia volumes as cargo is repositioned, potential spillover demand on CUL-served regional lanes (China–SE Asia, Taiwan connections). Monitor for cascading rate effects on Asia regional trades. The truce expiry (mid-July) is a key date — prepare for potential market disruption if tariffs are reinstated. CUL不运营跨太平洋直航，但关税驱动的激增影响亚洲支线网络：货物重新配置导致亚洲区域内运量增加，CUL服务区域航线（中国-东南亚、台湾连接）可能出现溢出需求。监控亚洲区域贸易的级联运价效应。休战到期（7月中旬）是关键日期——若关税恢复，需为潜在市场扰动做好准备。

Alphaliner WK21: US Indicts Four Asian Container Manufacturers for Price Fixing (2019–2024)
Alphaliner WK21：美国起诉四家亚洲集装箱制造商价格操纵（2019-2024）
Legal Equipment Global
The US DoJ unsealed indictments against CIMC, CXIC Group, Shanghai Universal Logistics (Dong Fang), and Singamas Container Holdings for colluding to fix dry container prices from 2019–2024. The alleged scheme involved output restrictions, shift limits, and production quotas agreed at a November 2019 meeting at CIMC's Shenzhen HQ. Container prices allegedly doubled from $1,600 to $3,500 during the period. Singamas CEO Teo Siong Seng (also PIL-linked) has taken leave; Marketing Director Vick Nam Hing was arrested in France pending extradition.
美国司法部公开起诉中集集团（CIMC）、CXIC集团、上海寰宇物流（东方国际）和胜狮货柜（Singamas），指控其2019-2024年间串通操纵干货箱价格。涉嫌方案包括产量限制、班次限制和生产配额——于2019年11月在中集深圳总部会议上达成。期间集装箱价格据称翻倍（$1,600→$3,500）。胜狮CEO张松声（与太平船务PIL关联）已休假；市场总监Vick Nam Hing在法国被捕等待引渡。
