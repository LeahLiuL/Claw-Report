⭐ US-China Trade Truce: 90-Day Tariff Pause Announced — Shipping Markets React with Surge in Bookings
⭐ 中美贸易休战：宣布90天关税暂停——航运市场订舱量激增回应
⭐ Featured ⚡ CUL Direct Commercial Trade Network Asia / Americas
The US and China announced a 90-day tariff truce on April 22, reducing US tariffs on Chinese goods from 145% to 30% and Chinese tariffs on US goods from 125% to 10% for the duration. The announcement triggered an immediate surge in trans-Pacific bookings: Vizion TradeView data shows China-US eastbound bookings up ~40% WoW as importers rush to front-load ahead of the truce expiry. Carriers are responding by reinstating previously blank-sailed services.
中美两国于4月22日宣布90天关税休战，期间美国对中国商品关税从145%降至30%，中国对美国商品关税从125%降至10%。公告触发跨太平洋订舱量立即激增：Vizion TradeView数据显示，中美东行订舱量周环比增长约40%，进口商抢在休战到期前提前备货。船公司正在恢复此前已停航的班次。
🎯 CUL Impact: Immediate opportunity window on transpacific. CUL commercial teams should reach out proactively to China-US trade customers — rate pressure will ease temporarily. However, this is a temporary truce; plan for potential reinstatement at truce expiry (mid-July). Secure medium-term contract positions while spot rates are relatively stable. Vietnam/SE Asia front-loading demand will partially reverse as China-direct becomes viable again. 跨太平洋市场出现即时机会窗口。CUL商务团队应主动联系中美贸易客户——运价压力将暂时缓解。但这是临时休战；需为休战到期（7月中旬）后可能重新加征做好预案。趁即期运价相对稳定时锁定中期合同头寸。随着中国直航重新可行，越南/东南亚前置备货需求将部分逆转。

The Drewry World Container Index (WCI) rose 4% this week (Week 17) to $2,340/FEU, snapping a two-week post-tariff correction. Shanghai–Los Angeles surged 7% to $3,006; Shanghai–Rotterdam up 3% to $2,296; Shanghai–New York up 3% to $3,659. The rebound was driven entirely by the US-China tariff truce announcement on April 22. SCFI composite rose 2.8% WoW to approximately 1,142 points.
Drewry世界集装箱运价指数（WCI）本周（第17周）上涨4%至$2,340/FEU，终止了两周的关税后回调。上海—洛杉矶大涨7%至$3,006；上海—鹿特丹涨3%至$2,296；上海—纽约涨3%至$3,659。此次反弹完全由4月22日中美关税休战公告驱动。SCFI综合指数周环比上涨约2.8%至约1,142点。
🎯 CUL Impact: Rate rebound creates a narrow window for CUL to revise spot rate offers upward on transpacific. However, the sustainability is uncertain — truce is 90 days only. ⭐ Red Sea / Hormuz Update: Ceasefire Holding But Carriers Maintain Cape Routing — Insurance Costs Still Elevated
⭐ 红海/霍尔木兹更新：停火维持但船公司继续好望角绕行——保险成本仍高
⭐ Featured ⚡ CUL Direct Ops Legal Network Middle East / Global
The US-Iran ceasefire continues to hold in Week 17, now entering its third week. However, all major carriers — including Maersk, MSC, CMA CGM, and Hapag-Lloyd — have maintained Cape of Good Hope routing for the time being, citing "insufficient security guarantees." War risk insurance premiums for Gulf transits remain at 0.5–0.7% of vessel value per voyage, vs. 0.1% pre-conflict. Hormuz transit monitoring shows approximately 420 vessels now stranded vs. 823 at peak. Panama Canal daily transits have fully recovered to 36–38 per day.
美伊停火进入第三周，第17周仍在维持。然而，所有主要船公司——包括马士基、MSC、达飞和赫伯罗特——仍以"安全保障不足"为由维持好望角绕行路由。海湾过境战争风险保险保费仍高达船舶价值的0.5-0.7%/航次，而冲突前为0.1%。霍尔木兹通行监控显示约420艘船舶仍被困（峰值为823艘）。巴拿马运河日均通行量已完全恢复至每天36-38艘次。
🎯 CUL Impact: Cape routing surcharges remain valid — do NOT withdraw EBS/EFS until carriers officially announce Suez resumption. Begin scoping Q3 re-routing planning now. 好望角绕行附加费仍有效——在船公司正式宣布苏伊士复航之前，不得撤销EBS/EFS。每周监控船公司苏伊士复航公告；准备好恢复正常路由时的更新运价表。巴拿马运河恢复降低了亚洲-美东溢价；重新评估CUL使用巴拿马航线的服务路由。立即着手Q3重新路由规划。
