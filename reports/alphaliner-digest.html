Drewry WCI Surges 12% to $2,553/FEU — Early Peak Season Rally Driven by Transpacific & Asia-Europe
Drewry WCI大涨12%至$2,553/FEU — 跨太平洋及亚欧航线拉动提前旺季行情
Title Story Freight Rates Global
The Drewry World Container Index (WCI) surged 12% to $2,553 per 40ft container for the week of May 14, 2026, snapping three consecutive weeks of decline. The rally was primarily driven by sharp increases on Transpacific and Asia-Europe trade routes, with carriers layering General Rate Increases (GRIs) and Emergency Bunker Surcharges (EBS) ahead of what appears to be an early peak season. Shanghai–Los Angeles rose 10% to $3,357/FEU; Shanghai–New York jumped 14% to $4,252/FEU. Carriers from MSC, Maersk, CMA CGM and Hapag-Lloyd all announced rate increase programmes for May.
截至2026年5月14日当周，德鲁里世界集装箱运价指数(WCI)大幅上涨12%至每40英尺箱2,553美元，终结此前连续三周下跌走势。本次反弹主要由跨太平洋和亚欧航线的运价急升推动，各船公司纷纷在看似提前到来的旺季前实施GRI和EBS。上海—洛杉矶上涨10%至3,357美元/FEU；上海—纽约上涨14%至4,252美元/FEU。MSC、马士基、达飞、赫伯罗特均宣布了5月涨价方案。

Strait of Hormuz — Partial Re-opening & Residual Risk
霍尔木兹海峡 — 部分重开与残余风险
Hormuz Strait Partially Reopens in May 2026 — But Vessel Transits Remain at 17/day vs. 125/day Pre-Crisis
霍尔木兹海峡5月部分重开 — 但船舶通行量仍仅为17艘/天，远低于危机前的125艘/天
Featured Geopolitical Risk Middle East
The Strait of Hormuz partially reopened in early May 2026 following months of severe restrictions, marking a positive development for global trade. However, vessel transits remain far below pre-crisis levels: on May 14, only 17 vessels transited the strait (12 inbound, 5 outbound), compared to an average of 125/day before the crisis. One of the 17 transiting vessels (~6%) crossed "dark" (AIS off), a Panamax bunkering tanker tracked southbound. Clarksons SIN data shows a gradual recovery from 7/day in March to 9/day in April and ~15–17/day in May so far. Container lines are still routing much of their Middle East and India/Pakistan cargo via Cape of Good Hope, maintaining elevated freight rates.
霍尔木兹海峡于2026年5月初在数月严重限制后部分重开，为全球贸易带来积极信号。然而，船舶通行量仍远低于危机前水平：5月14日仅有17艘船通过海峡（12艘入港，5艘出港），而危机前日均125艘。17艘中有1艘（约6%）以"暗船"（关闭AIS）方式通过，为一艘向南行驶的巴拿马型加油船。Clarksons SIN数据显示，通行量从3月的7艘/天、4月的9艘/天逐步恢复至5月的约15-17艘/天。集装箱船公司仍将大部分中东和印巴航线货物经好望角绕行，维持运价高位。
CUL Impact: Hormuz partial reopening is net positive for West Asia / Middle East / Persian Gulf trade (CUL's key markets). However, full normalization is not imminent. CUL's W. Asia port calls and Persian Gulf services remain subject to elevated war risk insurance premiums. CUL chartering team should continue monitoring the rerouting premium (typically +10–15 days voyage via Cape) when evaluating TC vs. voyage charter economics. Any reduction in Hormuz rerouting will compress CUL's effective capacity advantage on Middle East routes.
CUL影响评估：霍尔木兹海峡部分重开对西亚/中东/波斯湾贸易（CUL核心市场）总体利好。但完全正常化短期内尚无预期。CUL西亚港口挂靠和波斯湾服务仍面临较高的战争险保费。CUL租船团队在评估期租与航次租船经济性时，应持续关注绕行溢价（经好望角通常额外增加10-15天航期）。一旦霍尔木兹绕航减少，CUL在中东航线的有效运力优势将收窄。

The global bunker market entered a phase of relative stabilisation during Week 20, according to Container-News.com, although mixed price fluctuations continued across hubs.
