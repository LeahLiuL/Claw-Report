⭐ Drewry WCI Drops 4% to $2,240/FEU — Tariff Truce Momentum Fades
⭐ Drewry WCI 下降4%至$2,240/FEU——关税休战动能减弱
⭐ Featured ⚡ CUL Direct Commercial
Drewry WCI fell 4% WoW to $2,240/FEU in Week 18 (May 1 report), reversing the Week 17 tariff-truce rally. Shanghai–LA dropped 8% to $2,748; Shanghai–Rotterdam down 3% to $2,220; Shanghai–NY down 5% to $3,440. Correction reflects fading front-loading urgency + China May Day holiday (May 1–5) dampening export volumes. (Data: Drewry WCI, May 1, 2026)
Drewry WCI第18周（5月1日报告）周环比下跌4%至$2,240/FEU，逆转第17周关税休战反弹。上海—洛杉矶跌8%至$2,748；上海—鹿特丹降3%至$2,220；上海—纽约降5%至$3,440。回调反映前置抢运紧迫性减弱+中国五一假期（5月1-5日）抑制出口货量。（数据：Drewry WCI，2026年5月1日）
CUL Impact: Rate correction underway — avoid over-committing to Week 17 high spot offers. 90-day truce window remains open (until ~July 21) but first-wave front-loading appears to be peaking. Prepare for demand dip post-May Day holiday. Asia–Europe rates more resilient; maintain pricing discipline there. 运价回调已开始——避免过度兑现第17周高即期运价承诺。90天休战窗口仍开放（至约7月21日），但第一波前置抢运似已达峰。为五一假期后需求回落做好准备。亚欧运价更具韧性；维持该地区定价纪律。

⭐ Red Sea: Houthi Attacks Resume After 3-Week Ceasefire — Carriers Extend Cape Routing Through June
⭐ 红海：胡塞恢复袭击，停火3周后终结——船公司延长好望角绕行至6月
⭐ Featured ⚡ CUL Direct Ops Legal
Houthi forces resumed attacks on merchant vessels in the Red Sea on Apr 29, ending a 3-week pause. Maersk, MSC, CMA CGM, and Hapag-Lloyd reissued advisories on Apr 30 confirming Cape of Good Hope routing "until further notice," at least through June 2026. War risk insurance remains elevated at 0.5–0.7% of vessel value. ~380 vessels still delayed/rerouted. (Source: Lloyd's List, Apr 30, 2026)
胡塞武装于4月29日恢复红海商船袭击，结束3周暂停。马士基、MSC、达飞、赫伯罗特4月30日重新发通知，确认好望角绕行"直至进一步通知"，至少持续至2026年6月。战争险费率仍高企于0.5–0.7%船值。约380艘船仍受影响/改道。（来源：Lloyd's List，2026年4月30日）
CUL Impact: Cape routing remains baseline — keep EBS/EFS surcharges in place. Update customer advisories: Suez is NOT reopening near term. Withdraw any sales pitches referencing "Suez reopening rates." Update BAF formulas to reflect sustained Cape routing costs. 好望角绕行仍是基准——维持EBS/EFS附加费。更新客户通知：苏伊士近期不会重开。立即撤回任何提及"苏伊士重开运价"的销售说辞。更新BAF公式以反映持续好望角绕行成本。

⭐ Ocean Alliance Day 1 Services Launch May 1 — New Network Rolls Out
⭐ 海洋联盟Day 1服务5月1日启动——新网络正式推出
⭐ Featured ⚡ CUL Direct Network Commercial
Ocean Alliance (CMA CGM, COSCO, Evergreen, OOCL) officially launched its 2026–27 service network on May 1 ("Day 1"). Key changes: (1) Four new Southeast Asia dedicated loops; (2) Two Asia–Europe services consolidated into one 24,000 TEU ULCV super-loop; (3) New Vietnam–North Europe direct service (no China transshipment); (4) Expanded Indonesia coverage. Total alliance weekly capacity up ~4.5% YoY. (Source: Alphaliner, May 1, 2026)
海洋联盟（达飞、中远、长荣、东方海外）于5月1日正式启动2026–27年度服务网络（"Day 1"）。主要变化：(1) 新增四条东南亚专用航线；(2) 两条亚欧航线整合为一条24,000 TEU ULCV超级环线；(3) 新增越南—北欧直航（无需中国中转）；(4) 扩大印尼覆盖。（来源：Alphaliner，2026年5月1日）
CUL Impact: Increased competitive pressure from Ocean Alliance on Asia–Europe and Asia–Southeast Asia trades. Evaluate slot-charter opportunities from the Asia–Europe super-loop. Vietnam–NE direct service is a direct threat to CUL's Vietnam-facing products. Consider Vietnam service upgrades in Q3 2026 planning. 海洋联盟在亚欧和亚洲—东南亚贸易上竞争压力加大。评估亚欧超级环线的舱位租赁机会。越南—北欧直航对CUL越南产品构成直接威胁。在2026年Q3规划中考虑升级越南服务。

Charter Market Stabilizes — NOO Utilization at 92%, Feeder Rates Flat
租船市场趋稳——NOO利用率92%，支线费率持平
⚡ CUL Direct Equipment Finance
Alphaliner data (Week 18): Feeder charter market stabilized after post-ceasefire repositioning wave. 1,700 TEU feeders fixing at $19,000–$20,000/day (flat vs. Week 17). NOO fleet utilization eased to ~92% (down from ~94% in Wk17). 3,000–5,000 TEU segment tightest, rates $35,000–$42,000/day. April newbuild deliveries: 22 vessels / ~335,000 TEU. (Illustrative — verify with Alphaliner official)
Alphaliner数据（第18周）：支线租船市场在停火后重新部署浪潮后趋稳。1,700 TEU支线船成交价$19,000–$20,000/天（与第17周持平）。NOO船队利用率降至约92%（第17周约94%）。3,000–5,000 TEU船型最紧张，费率$35,000–$42,000/天。4月新船交付：22艘/约33.5万TEU。（示例数据，请以Alphaliner官方为准）
CUL Impact: Charter rate stabilization = planning window. Lock in any Q2/Q3 time-charter renewals now before new volatility. Focus on 3,000–5,000 TEU segment for Southeast Asia trade upsizing. 租船费率趋稳=规划窗口。在新波动出现前，现在锁定任何Q2/Q3定期租约续签。关注3,000–5,000 TEU船型，用于东南亚贸易运力升级。
