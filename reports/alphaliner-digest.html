Drewry WCI Extends Rally +6% to $2,712/FEU — Asia-Europe Surge Leads; Two Consecutive Weeks of Strong Gains
Drewry WCI延续涨势+6%至$2,712/FEU — 亚欧航线引领，连续两周强劲上涨
Title Story Freight Rates Global
The Drewry World Container Index (WCI) extended its rally for the second consecutive week, rising 6% to $2,712 per 40ft container for the week of May 21, 2026 — the highest level since the peak season of early 2024. The primary driver this week was a sharp jump on Asia-Europe trade: Shanghai–Rotterdam surged ~16% to approximately $3,650/FEU. Transpacific gains were more modest: Shanghai–New York grew 2% to $4,317/FEU; Shanghai–Los Angeles edged up 1% to $3,385/FEU. Combined, the two-week WCI gain of +19% (from $2,286 post-Week 20 trough) signals that early peak season demand is real. SCFI composite (May 22) reached 2,140.66, up 9.54% on the week, with Mediterranean routes the standout at +27.69%.
德鲁里世界集装箱运价指数(WCI)连续第二周延续涨势，2026年5月21日当周上涨6%至每40英尺箱2,712美元，为2024年初旺季以来最高水平。本周主要驱动力来自亚欧贸易的急涨：上海—鹿特丹上涨约16%至约3,650美元/FEU。跨太平洋涨幅相对温和：上海—纽约上涨2%至4,317美元/FEU；上海—洛杉矶小涨1%至3,385美元/FEU。综合来看，连续两周WCI累计涨幅达+19%，发出旺季提前到来的明确信号。SCFI综合指数（5月22日）达2,140.66，周涨幅9.54%，其中地中海航线表现最为突出，涨幅高达+27.69%。

Strait of Hormuz — Chinese VLCCs Signal Gradual Re-opening
霍尔木兹海峡 — 中国VLCC公开AIS通行，发出逐步开放信号
Two Chinese-Controlled VLCCs Transit Hormuz Openly (AIS On) May 20 — First Confirmed Normal Transit in Weeks
5月20日两艘中国控制VLCC开启AIS公开通过霍尔木兹 — 数周来首次确认正常通行
Featured Geopolitical Middle East
On May 20, 2026, two Chinese-controlled VLCCs transited the Strait of Hormuz outbound in a tight 5–10 km formation, openly broadcasting AIS — marking the first confirmed normal (non-dark) commercial transit in weeks. This is a significant shift from previous weeks, where the majority of transiting vessels (including a Panamax bunkering tanker tracked in Week 21) crossed "dark" (AIS off). The Iran-US ceasefire, extended multiple times since April 7, continues to hold tenuously. Iran imposed formal new transit rules for vessels via the strait (CNN reported May 7), requiring registration and passage fees. Container lines have largely maintained Cape of Good Hope routings for Middle East cargo, but this development could accelerate normalization discussions in the coming weeks.
2026年5月20日，两艘中国控制的VLCC以5-10公里近距编队方式，公开开启AIS通过霍尔木兹海峡出港，这是数周来首次确认的正常（非暗船）商业通行。与此前形成对比的是，第21周大多数通行船舶（含一艘追踪到的巴拿马型加油船）均以"暗船"（关闭AIS）方式通过。美伊停火协议自4月7日以来已多次延期，目前仍处于脆弱维持状态。伊朗于5月7日（CNN报道）正式颁布新海峡通行规则，要求船舶登记并缴纳通行费。集装箱船公司仍大多维持中东货物经好望角绕行，但这一进展或将加速未来数周的正常化讨论。
CUL Impact: Any normalization of Hormuz transits is net-negative for CUL's effective capacity advantage derived from Cape rerouting. CUL chartering should model two scenarios: (a) Hormuz stays restricted — current charter market stays firm, Cape routing premium maintained; (b) Hormuz fully reopens — significant extra tonnage (~4-6 weeks voyage absorbed) returns to market, potential rate softening H2 2026. CUL West Asia port calls and Persian Gulf services: war risk insurance premiums remain elevated despite AIS transit, averaging 0.5–1% of hull value per voyage. Monitor closely for full normalization triggers.
CUL影响评估：霍尔木兹通行正常化对CUL因好望角绕行所获得的有效运力优势净负面。CUL租船团队应建立两种情景模型：(a)霍尔木兹持续受限——当前租船市场维持强劲，好望角绕行溢价持续；(b)霍尔木兹完全开放——大量额外运力（约4-6周航期被吸收的运力）重返市场，2026年下半年运价面临软化风险。CUL西亚港口挂靠及波斯湾服务：尽管AIS通行已出现，战争险保费仍处高位，每次航程平均约占船壳价值0.5-1%。请密切关注完全正常化的触发信号。

Singapore, Malaysia, India, Sri Lanka Transshipment Hubs Report Congestion Driven by Hormuz Rerouting & Peak Demand
新加坡、马来西亚、印度、斯里兰卡转运枢纽因霍尔木兹绕行及旺季需求报告拥堵
Port Ops SE Asia / S. Asia
Multiple Asian transshipment hubs are experiencing increased congestion in Week 22, driven by a combination of Cape of Good Hope rerouting (longer dwell times for Middle East cargo) and early peak season box demand. Singapore (PSA) reports berth utilization above 90% at key container terminals.
