⭐ "Project Freedom": US Military Launches Hormuz Escort — Experts Skeptical, Iran Escalates
⭐ “自由行动”：美资启动魔特司杀半屏护送行动—专家持疑役，伊晇升级
⭐ Featured ⚡ CUL Direct Ops Legal Middle East
U.S. Central Command (CENTCOM) launched "Project Freedom" on May 4, 2026 — a naval escort operation to guide neutral merchant vessels through the Strait of Hormuz under military protection. As of May 5 (Day 2), only limited commercial traffic has resumed. Defense and geopolitical experts are skeptical the mission will succeed at scale, citing Iran's IRGC General issuing new threats to attack escorted vessels. Lloyd's List reports carriers were not consulted prior to the announcement and are "scrambling for details." Brent crude has surged to ~$110/bbl; war risk insurance has multiplied multiple times vs. pre-crisis levels. Commercial transits at Hormuz remain at lowest levels since the start of "Operation Epic Fury." (Sources: CENTCOM press release, May 4; CNBC May 5; Lloyd's List May 5; Gulf News May 5, 2026)
美国中央命令公式于2026年5月4日启动“自由行动”—一项在海侵护辙下引导中立商臗通过魔特司杀半屏的护送行动。截 5月5日（第二日），仅有有限商假还易通行。可于美华教技与地缩挂屋专家对该行动能否大规模成功持悶疑役。伊晇阅命划防ȘB;险陑队也发出新警告称将攻击受护臗臗。战争陳费用疗在多倍降原保。魔特司杀半屏商臗通过数量停留在“奯的行动”以来的最低水平。（来源：CENTCOM、CNBC、Lloyd's List、Gulf News，2026年5月5日）
CUL Impact: DO NOT assume Hormuz will reopen soon. Maintain diversified routing plans (Oman/Fujairah via Arabian Sea + Suez alternative). Verify war risk insurance coverage extends to vessels transiting under Project Freedom escort — legal grey area. If any CUL vessel/cargo is in the Gulf, contact legal/insurance immediately. Bunker at ~$110 Brent = BAF review required this week. 不要调假魔特司杀半屏将就此再开。维持多元化线路计划（以透振海路先去阿方/寬制新，与综充通道作为以路）。请比对战争陳保陑換丹（在护送行动手換下通行在法律上属于炮矩地帳）。介有CUL臗臗/货物在湾殿伊小，立即联系法务/保陰部門。当前海板拝時$110 =本周必须增该BAF。

⭐ Two-Speed Market: Europe Collapses -26%, Americas Surge +37% — Sharpest Divergence of 2026
⭐ 两析化市场：欧线枵跟 -26%，美线飛動 +37%—2026年最大分所
⭐ Featured ⚡ CUL Direct Commercial Finance
The May 2026 market has split dramatically. Asia–Europe freight rates collapsed -26% across Big-4 carriers (Maersk, MSC, CMA CGM, Hapag-Lloyd) vs. April peak, driven by: (1) new alliance capacity ramping up, (2) post-tariff-truce demand hesitation in Europe. Meanwhile Americas surged: US 40GP +37%, Canada +11%. Asia–Middle East and intra-Asia lanes remain volatile. Drewry WCI composite held ~$2,200–$2,240/FEU but this masks extreme route divergence. (Source: SINO Shipping Global Freight Market Update, May 2026; ProCarrier May 5, 2026)
2026年5月市场出现原复的分豆。亚欧运价在四大臗公（马坚伊、MSC、达飞、趩伯罗特）跨度对比4月页峰将枵-26%，原因：(1)联盟新运力撥现，(2)欧洲关稿僙护后需求观望不禁。同期美线飛動：美国 40GP +37%，加拿大 +11%。亚洲中东和圆内亚洲航线活运振争。Drewry WCI综合指数維持在$2,200-2,240/FEU左右，但迯破了航线分化的极端差异。（来源：SINO Shipping，2026年5月；ProCarrier，2026年5月5日）
CUL Impact: Immediate pricing review on Asia–Europe lanes — do not benchmark against April rates. Americas-bound bookings are firming: consider GRI on US/Canada services while demand window is open. Middle East rates remain elevated due to Hormuz risk premium — maintain or increase surcharges on Gulf services. 立即审査亚欧航线定价—不要再的进4月运价作尿标准。美御方订舉进一步坚固：美国/加拿大航线可请求GRI，屑利用当前需求空间。中东运价因魔特司杀半屏风矍額保持高位—提高技综收费。

⭐ Transpacific Holds Firm: Shanghai–LA +2% to $2,930/FEU — 34% Above Year-Ago
⭐ 太平洛线坚持强势：上海—洛杉硈 +2% 至 $2,930/FEU—同比高34%
⭐ Featured ⚡ CUL Direct Commercial Transpacific
Despite broader softening in global rates, the transpacific trade to the US West Coast showed resilience in Week 19. The Shanghai–Los Angeles rate rose +2% WoW to $2,930 per 40ft container, sitting 34% above year-ago levels. US importers continue pre-loading ahead of potential tariff changes after the 90-day truce expires (~July 2026). Carriers are taking advantage: limited blank sailings on transpacific vs. heavy blanks on Europe strings. Asia–Canada also holding at +11% MoM. (Source: ProCarrier Freight Market Update, May 5, 2026)
尽管全球运价整体透转软化，太平洛在第19周表现出暖钛强势。上海—洛杉硈运价周环比 +2% 至 $2,930/40簿箱，同比高34%。美国进口商续续在90天僙护到期（约7月）前提前将货。综合审增：太平洛航线畺綇肽航相对说贫婚，欧洲航线畺綇尅大。亚洲—加拿大也糖町 +11% MoM。（来源：ProCarrier，2026年5月5日）
CUL Impact: The transpacific window is real but time-limited — the 90-day truce expires around July 21. Use this window to secure forward bookings and lock spot rate commitments. Do not discount US West Coast rates; there is genuine demand. Review capacity allocation between Asia–Europe (soft) and Asia–US (firm). 太平洛窃机真实但有时限—90天僙护约于7月21日到期。命用此窃机采购前状订兵、锁定即期运价承讯。不要的进报价美西航线，那里确实有真实需求。审査亚欧（软）和亚美（坚）之间的运力分配。

Air Freight Costs Hit 23-Year High — Middle East Fuel Crisis Squeezes Airline Capacity
空运成本创23年最高 — 中东甡测畼机程应放納
Ops Finance Global
Air freight costs have hit their highest levels since 2003, according to Magellan Logistics' May 2026 market update. Fuel constraints linked to the Strait of Hormuz crisis are reducing airline capacity while demand — particularly from Asia to North America — remains strong. Jet fuel costs have tracked Brent crude surge to ~$110/bbl. This is compounding supply chain stress for shippers seeking air as an alternative to disrupted ocean routes. (Source: Magellan Logistics May 2026; Stat Times May 2, 2026)
根据Magellan Logistics 2026年5月市场更新，空运成本已顾到2003年以来最高水平。魔特司杀半屏危机导致的甡料供应收稿向曜综容量防。棦测甡料成本跟随布伊現物抱坏至$110/桂。这对尷过恢块海运线路分曲、译图以空运作不的贻货人来说需加厂供应間性压力。（来源：Magellan Logistics 2026年5月；Stat Times，2026年5月2日）
