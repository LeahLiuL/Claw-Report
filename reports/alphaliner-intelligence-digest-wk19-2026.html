⭐ Hormuz Stays Restricted — Permission Corridor Active, ~2,000 Vessels Stranded; US Launches "Project Freedom"
⭐ 霍尔木兹海峡持续管控——"许可走廊"运行，约2,000艘船只滞留；美国启动"自由行动"
⭐ Featured ⚡ CUL Direct Ops Legal Network Middle East / Global
The Strait of Hormuz remains under Iran's "permission-based corridor" system following the February 28 crisis. Only 3 container ships have successfully transited since conflict began. As of May 5, approximately 2,000 vessels and 20,000 crew remain stranded in the Arabian Gulf. Iran charges over $1 million per authorized vessel transit. UK Parliamentary Library analysis: 50% pre-crisis transit levels unlikely before August 2026. The US launched Operation Project Freedom on May 4 to pressure for resumed commercial passage.
自2月28日危机爆发以来，霍尔木兹海峡持续实行伊朗的"许可走廊"制度。开战至今仅3艘集装箱船成功过境。截至5月5日，约2,000艘船只和20,000名船员仍滞留阿拉伯湾。伊朗对每艘获授权过境船只收取逾100万美元通行费。英国议会图书馆分析：8月前将通行量恢复至危机前50%水平希望渺茫。美国于5月4日启动"自由行动"（Project Freedom），向伊朗施压以恢复商业通行。
🎯 CUL Impact: Hormuz blockage continues to force all CUL Middle East voyages (JEA, Dammam, Muscat, Sohar) via Cape of Good Hope at est. $300–500K extra fuel/voyage. The global fleet is absorbing ~2.5M TEU capacity on extended Cape routing, keeping charter rates firm. Monitor Project Freedom developments — any signal of corridor reopening would dramatically reset Middle East routing and surcharge levels. CUL should maintain current Cape routing and surcharge regime until confirmed softening. 霍尔木兹封锁持续迫使CUL所有中东航次（杰贝阿里、达曼、马斯喀特、苏哈尔）绕行好望角，估计额外燃油成本$30-50万/航次。全球船队因延长绕行好望角约吸收250万TEU运力，使租船费率保持坚挺。密切关注"自由行动"进展——任何海峡重开信号都将大幅重置中东路由和附加费水平。在确认缓和前，CUL应维持当前绕行安排和附加费制度。

⭐ India-Pakistan Port Ban — MSC Launches Colombo Shuttle, CMA CGM Issues EORS Surcharge
⭐ 印巴港口互禁——MSC开通科伦坡穿梭服务，CMA CGM推出EORS附加费
⭐ Featured ⚡ CUL Direct Network Legal Trade South Asia
India has banned Pakistani-flagged vessels and containers originating from Pakistan; Pakistan has retaliated with a symmetric ban. Pakistan's Karachi and Qasim ports face severe export cargo backlogs. MSC launched an emergency Pakistan–Colombo shuttle service to keep Pakistan exporters connected to global trade. CMA CGM introduced an Emergency Operations Recovery Surcharge (EORS) of up to $800/box effective May 15 for cargo via alternative hubs to USA, Latin America, and Australia.
印度禁止巴基斯坦籍船舶及来自巴基斯坦的集装箱货物入境；巴基斯坦以对等禁令回应。卡拉奇和卡西姆港出口货物积压严重。MSC推出紧急巴基斯坦-科伦坡穿梭服务，确保巴基斯坦出口商与全球贸易的连通。CMA CGM推出紧急运营恢复附加费（EORS），5月15日起对经替代枢纽至美国、拉美、澳大利亚的货物征收最高800美元/箱。
🎯 CUL Impact: CUL operates services calling South Asia (Nhava Sheva, Mundra, Colombo). The India-Pakistan ban creates both risk and opportunity: (1) Colombo hub volumes likely to increase sharply as Pakistani cargo reroutes; (2) Assess whether CUL has any Pakistan-origin cargo on India-calling services that may now require rerouting via Sri Lanka or Oman; (3) Commercial team to confirm CUL's legal exposure on Indo-Pak bilateral service restrictions; (4) Colombo transshipment uplift — review yard capacity and berthing slots at CUL's Colombo partner terminals. CUL在南亚运营挂靠服务（那瓦舍瓦、蒙德拉、科伦坡）。印巴禁令既带来风险也带来机会：(1) 巴基斯坦货物改道后科伦坡枢纽量将大幅增加；(2) 评估CUL印度挂靠航线上是否有巴基斯坦来源货物需经斯里兰卡或阿曼改道；(3) 商务团队确认CUL在印巴双边服务限制上的法律风险敞口；(4) 科伦坡转运量上升——审查CUL科伦坡合作码头的堆场容量和泊位情况。

⚡ CUL Direct Finance Trade Asia / Americas
CMA CGM rolled out a Peak Season Surcharge (PSS) of $2,000 per 40-foot container effective May 1. MSC simultaneously raised its Emergency Fuel Surcharge (EFS) for Asia–US East Coast from $430/FEU to $644/FEU (+$214, or +49.8%). War risk surcharges continue to stack on top of base rates, pushing effective all-in rates significantly higher. Carriers are aggressively defending May–June yield ahead of expected capacity additions in H2 2026.

🎯 CUL Impact: PSS/EFS surcharge push from major carriers creates pricing umbrella for CUL's rate actions on Asia–Americas and Middle East–Americas corridors. Review CUL's own May–June surcharge schedule to ensure alignment with market. Transpacific cargo surge from US tariff front-loading is winding down — monitor volume trajectories carefully before extending aggressive rates into Q3. 大型船公司的PSS/EFS附加费推涨为CUL在亚洲-美洲及中东-美洲走廊的运价调整提供了定价保护伞。审查CUL自身5-6月附加费计划，确保与市场保持同步。受美国关税抢运潮拖动的跨太平洋货量正逐渐回落——在将积极运价策略延伸至Q3前，应密切监控货量走势。
