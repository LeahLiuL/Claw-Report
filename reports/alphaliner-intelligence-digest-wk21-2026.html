⭐ Featured ⚡ CUL Direct Ops Network Legal Middle East / Global
President Trump stated on May 24 that a deal with Iran — including the opening of the Strait of Hormuz — has been "largely negotiated," following calls with Israeli and Gulf allies. US and Iranian negotiators have signaled progress on uranium enrichment and transit tolls, but key sticking points remain. CNN live updates confirm both sides are "closer than ever" to a framework agreement. 特朗普5月24日表示，与伊朗达成协议——包括开放霍尔木兹海峡——已"基本谈妥"，此前他与以色列及海湾盟友通话。美伊谈判人员在铀浓缩和过境费方面均发出积极信号，但关键分歧依然存在。CNN直播更新确认双方"比以往任何时候都更接近"框架协议。5月20日，两艘中国控制的VLCC在开放AIS广播下紧密编队驶出霍尔木兹，显示伊朗可能正在选择性放松管控以预设协议空间。

⭐ Drewry WCI +6% to $2,712 — Third Consecutive Weekly Rise; Asia-Europe Leads Surge
⭐ Drewry WCI 周涨6%至$2,712——连续第三周上涨，亚欧领涨
⭐ Featured Finance Commercial Trade Global
The Drewry World Container Index (WCI) rose 6% this week to $2,712/40ft — the third consecutive weekly increase and the strongest multi-week rally since early 2024. The surge is driven primarily by Asia–Europe rates, with Shanghai–Rotterdam up sharply, boosted by peak season demand, GRI/PSS implementation by major carriers (Hapag-Lloyd, MSC, CMA CGM), and persistent Cape of Good Hope detour costs. Shanghai–New York grew 2% to $4,317 and Shanghai–LA rose 1% to $3,385. Drewry notes the market has now confirmed a Q2 rate recovery reversal from the Wk19 trough.
Drewry WCI本周上涨6%至$2,712/40尺——连续第三周上涨，是2024年初以来最强多周涨势。主要驱动力来自亚欧运价大幅攀升，峰季需求、主要船公司（赫伯罗特、MSC、达飞）落地GRI/PSS以及好望角绕行成本居高不下共同推动。上海→纽约涨2%至$4,317，→洛杉矶涨1%至$3,385。Drewry指出市场已确认从第19周低点反转进入Q2运价回升通道。
