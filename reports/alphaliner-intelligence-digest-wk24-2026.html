Peak Season Arrives 4–6 Weeks Early — What's Driving the Surge?
旺季提前4–6周到来——驱动力分析
Commercial Network Finance Global
The 2026 container shipping peak season has arrived 4–6 weeks earlier than the traditional July–August window. Three key drivers: (1) US inventory restocking cycle after destocking in H2 2025; (2) Front-loading of cargo ahead of potential new US tariff measures; (3) Capacity absorption from Hormuz-related Cape of Good Hope diversions removing ~8–10% of effective global capacity. Combined with carrier blank sailing programs, the market is experiencing the tightest supply-demand balance since the COVID-era surge.
2026年集装箱航运旺季较传统7–8月窗口提前4–6周到来。三大驱动力：（1）美国在2025下半年去库存后进入补库周期；（2）潜在新关税措施前的货物抢运；（3）霍尔木兹相关好望角绕航吸收全球有效运力约8–10%。叠加船公司空白航班计划，市场正经历自疫情时期以来最紧张的供需平衡。
