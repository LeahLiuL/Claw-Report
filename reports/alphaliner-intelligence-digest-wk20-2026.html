⭐ Drewry WCI Surges 12% to $2,553 — Peak Season Surcharges Stack Across All Major Carriers
⭐ Drewry WCI飙升12%至$2,553 — 主要船公司全面叠加峰季附加费
⭐ Featured ⚡ CUL Direct Finance Trade Global
The Drewry World Container Index (WCI) surged 12% to $2,553 per 40ft container in Week 20, driven by simultaneous Peak Season Surcharges (PSS) and Emergency Fuel Surcharges (EFS) implemented by MSC, CMA CGM, Maersk, Hapag-Lloyd, and Evergreen across Asia-origin trades. Transpacific and Asia–Europe routes led the gains. CMA CGM applied a $2,000/FEU PSS on Asia origins effective May 1. The market is entering an early peak season with tight spot availability.
德鲁里世界集装箱运价指数（WCI）第20周飙升12%至每40尺箱2,553美元，由MSC、达飞、马士基、赫伯罗特和长荣同步推出的峰季附加费（PSS）和紧急燃油附加费（EFS）驱动。跨太平洋和亚欧航线涨幅领先。达飞自5月1日起对亚洲出发货物征收2,000美元/FEU的PSS。市场正进入提前峰季，现货舱位趋紧。
🎯 CUL Impact: WCI +12% in a single week signals significant upward momentum. CUL should leverage PSS/EFS increases on spot bookings and review contract rates to ensure they incorporate peak season clauses. Priority: lock in tonnage for Q3 before further rate escalation. Assess if current contract rates are below market — renegotiation opportunity window is open. WCI单周+12%标志着显著上行动能。CUL应在现货订舱上杠杆利用PSS/EFS上涨，并审查合同运价以确保含峰季条款。优先事项：在费率进一步上涨前锁定Q3舱位。评估当前合同运价是否低于市场价——重谈机会窗口已开启。

⭐ Hormuz Remains Blocked — US Military Opens Escort Corridor but 1,500 Vessels Still Stranded
⭐ 霍尔木兹仍遭封锁 — 美军开辟护航通道，但仍有1,500艘船只受困
⭐ Featured ⚡ CUL Direct Ops Legal Network Middle East / Global
As of Week 20, approximately 1,500 ships and 20,000 crew members remain stranded near the Strait of Hormuz following continued US-Iranian clashes. On May 4, US military forces sank six Iranian small boats threatening commercial vessels and opened a limited escort corridor. However, the ceasefire remains fragile — Iran launched drone and missile attacks on the UAE on May 4, and the UN continues to urge vessels to avoid the strait. Most major carriers have not resumed normal Gulf operations.
截至第20周，约1,500艘船舶、20,000名船员在持续的美伊冲突后仍被困于霍尔木兹海峡附近。5月4日，美军击沉6艘威胁商业船只的伊朗小艇并开辟了有限护航通道。但停火协议仍然脆弱——伊朗5月4日对阿联酋发动无人机和导弹袭击，联合国继续建议船只避开该海峡。大多数主要船公司尚未恢复正常海湾运营。
🎯 CUL Impact: Hormuz crisis entering Week 12 with no clear resolution. CUL Middle East voyages continue on Cape of Good Hope routing — est. $300–500K excess fuel cost per voyage. US escort corridor provides partial relief but is limited capacity. MarineLink reported (May 8) that Maersk, Hapag-Lloyd, and CMA CGM are maintaining their Cape of Good Hope rerouting, continuing to bypass both the Suez Canal and Bab el-Mandeb Strait. The extra steaming distance adds approximately 14 days to Asia–Europe voyages and significantly increases fuel consumption.

🎯 CUL Impact: CUL is directly affected as the Cape routing impacts all Asia–Middle East–Europe voyages. Confirm tonnage availability for Q3 Q4 — Cape routing absorbs significantly more vessel capacity globally. CUL直接受影响，好望角航线影响所有亚洲-中东-欧洲航次。随着顶级船公司锁定好望角路线，CUL应一致执行相应的航次附加费。确认Q3/Q4船期可用性——好望角路线在全球吸收了更多的船舶运力。
