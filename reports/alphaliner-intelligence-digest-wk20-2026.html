⭐ Drewry WCI Surges +12% WoW to $2,553 — Early Peak Season + Double-Digit Rate Jumps on Key Lanes
⭐ Drewry WCI周涨12%至$2,553——早峰期+主要航线双位数涨幅
⭐ Featured ⚡ CUL Direct Trade Finance Global
The Drewry World Container Index rose 12% this week to US$2,553 per 40ft container — the largest single-week jump since early 2024. This was driven by an early peak season push and Emergency Fuel Surcharges (EFS) + Peak Season Surcharges (PSS) implemented simultaneously across major carriers. Shanghai→New York surged +14% to $4,252; Shanghai→LA +10% to $3,357; Shanghai→Rotterdam +20% (the week's largest route jump); Shanghai→Genoa rose sharply as Mediterranean rates rebounded. The index had previously risen 3% in Week 19 (to $2,286) — the surge in Wk20 marks a significant acceleration.
德鲁里世界集装箱指数本周上涨12%至每40尺箱2,553美元，为2024年初以来单周最大涨幅。驱动因素为提前到来的旺季需求叠加各大船公司同步实施紧急燃油附加费（EFS）和旺季附加费（PSS）。上海→纽约+14%至$4,252；上海→洛杉矶+10%至$3,357；上海→鹿特丹+20%（本周各航线最大涨幅）；上海→热那亚大幅上涨，地中海运价全面反弹。第19周指数已上涨3%至$2,286，第20周的加速上涨标志着市场进入新的强劲阶段。
🎯 CUL Impact: Strong rate environment benefits CUL revenue on Asia–Middle East, Asia–Europe and intra-Asia lanes. CUL should ensure PSS/EFS surcharges are applied consistently on all spot cargo. Review long-term contract rates — current spot vs. contract gap is widening. Transpacific surge is indirect but signals Asia cargo volumes are strong, which benefits feeder connections at Shanghai/Ningbo/Qingdao. Recommend accelerating GRI application for May–June sailing windows. 强劲的运价环境有利于CUL在亚洲-中东、亚洲-欧洲和亚洲内部航线的营收。CUL应确保在所有现货货物上一致应用PSS/EFS附加费。重新审视长期合同运价——当前即期与合同运价差距正在扩大。跨太平洋涨势间接表明亚洲货量强劲，有利于上海/宁波/青岛的支线接驳。建议加快推进5-6月装载期的GRI申请。

Major carriers including MSC, CMA CGM, Evergreen, Yang Ming, and Hapag-Lloyd implemented Emergency Fuel Surcharges (EFS) and Peak Season Surcharges (PSS) simultaneously effective May 1–15, 2026. This "surcharge stacking" is a key driver of the Drewry WCI +12% spike. On Asia–US West Coast: PSS averaging $500–800/FEU; EFS $200–400/FEU depending on carrier. Asia-Med carriers applied GRIs of $500–$1,000/40ft. Shippers are responding with cargo acceleration, further amplifying peak season dynamics.
MSC、达飞、长荣、阳明、赫伯罗特等主要船公司2026年5月1-15日同步实施紧急燃油附加费（EFS）和旺季附加费（PSS）。这种"附加费叠加"是德鲁里WCI周涨12%的主要驱动因素。亚洲-美西航线：PSS平均$500-800/FEU；EFS $200-400/FEU（因船公司而异）。亚洲-地中海船公司实施$500-$1,000/40尺的GRI。货主以加速货物预订回应，进一步放大旺季动态。

Verify all spot bookings post-May 1 include current EFS levels. CUL应确保自身附加费计划具有竞争力并完整执行5-6月装载期。EFS直接关系到燃油成本回收。验证5月1日后所有现货订舱均包含最新EFS水平。大型船公司的附加费叠加为CUL在其主要贸易航线（中东、亚洲内部）推涨运价创造了有利环境。现在就行动，趁市场再度回落前把握机会。
