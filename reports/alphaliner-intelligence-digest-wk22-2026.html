⭐ Drewry WCI Surges 6% to $2,712 — Third Consecutive Weekly Rise; Asia–Europe Leads with +15%
⭐ Drewry WCI大涨6%至$2,712——连续第三周上涨；亚欧领涨+15%
⭐ Featured ⚡ CUL Direct Finance Ops Network Global
The Drewry World Container Index (WCI) jumped 6% to $2,712/FEU for the week of May 21, marking its third consecutive weekly increase in May. The surge was primarily driven by Asia–Europe routes, where Shanghai–Rotterdam spot rates climbed 15% as early peak season demand kicked in and carriers enforced higher FAK levels. Shanghai–New York rose 7% to $3,310, while Shanghai–Los Angeles gained 4%. SCFI composite stands at 2,140.66. Drewry noted that peak season dynamics are running approximately 3–4 weeks ahead of historical norms.
Drewry世界集装箱指数（WCI）在5月21日当周大幅上涨6%至$2,712/FEU，创下5月份连续第三周上涨纪录。此轮上涨主要由亚欧航线驱动，上海-鹿特丹现货运价飙升15%，随着提前到来的峰季需求以及船公司执行更高FAK价位。上海-纽约上涨7%至$3,310，上海-洛杉矶上涨4%。SCFI综合指数报2,140.66。Drewry指出峰季动态比历史规律提前3-4周到来。
🎯 CUL Impact: WCI third-weekly-rise in a row signals early peak season is firmly established. CUL commercial team should accelerate FAK/GRI negotiations for June–August contracts on Asia–Europe and Asia–Med corridors. ⭐ Iran Establishes Persian Gulf Strait Authority (PGSA) — Formal Transit Permit System for Hormuz
⭐ 伊朗成立波斯湾海峡管理局（PGSA）——正式建立霍尔木兹过境许可制度
⭐ Featured ⚡ CUL Direct Legal Ops Network Middle East / Global
Iran formally established the Persian Gulf Strait Authority (PGSA) on May 5, 2026, asserting regulatory control over the Strait of Hormuz. All vessels must now obtain a transit permit before crossing. On May 16, Iran confirmed it will unveil a transit toll mechanism. Vessels are required to adjust operations per the new framework, including cargo nationality declarations and voyage plan submissions. Western carriers remain cautious — several have applied for permits with mixed results. The PGSA's 12-article statute gives Iran unprecedented control over one of the world's most critical chokepoints.
伊朗于2026年5月5日正式成立波斯湾海峡管理局（PGSA），对霍尔木兹海峡实施监管控制。所有船舶过境前必须取得过境许可证。5月16日，伊朗确认将公布过境收费机制。船舶须按新框架调整运营，包括货物国籍申报和航次计划提交。西方船公司保持谨慎——多家已申请许可证，结果参差不齐。PGSA的12条法规赋予伊朗对全球最重要咽喉要道之一的前所未有的控制权。
🎯 CUL Impact: PGSA formalization is a structural change for Middle East services. CUL legal team must assess permit requirements for any vessels calling Gulf ports (Jebel Ali, Dammam, Sohar). Anticipate transit toll costs once unveiled — estimate $50,000–$150,000 per transit based on comparable frameworks. Continued Cape of Good Hope detour remains viable for European-origin/destination calls. CUL commercial team should factor Hormuz toll premium into FAK/surcharge structures for Gulf-bound cargo. PGSA正式化是中东服务的结构性变化。CUL法务团队必须评估挂靠海湾港口（杰贝阿里、达曼、苏哈尔）船舶的许可证要求。一旦公布，预计过境收费成本——基于同类框架估计每次过境$5–15万。继续绕好望角对欧洲起/到港航次仍可行。CUL商务团队应将霍尔木兹通行费溢价纳入海湾向货物的FAK/附加费结构中。

Late April saw Rotterdam VLSFO at approximately $770/MT, but prices have risen sharply in May. Fujairah remains expensive and supply-constrained. Carriers are accelerating the introduction of Bunker Adjustment Factors (BAF) and Emergency Bunker Surcharges (EBS) to offset escalating fuel costs, with the war risk layer adding approximately $50–80/MT above historical norms.
