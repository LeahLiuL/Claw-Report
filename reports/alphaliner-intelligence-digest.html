Title Story Middle East Capacity Risk
Two months after the Strait of Hormuz effectively closed to commercial shipping, Kpler container intelligence data (published 15 May 2026) reveals the extent of the crisis: 53 container vessels belonging to major global liner companies were trapped in the Persian Gulf. As of Week 20, 42 of those vessels (79%) remain stranded and unable to exit. This is not temporary congestion — it represents a structural capacity freeze. Unlike the Red Sea diversions where vessels could reroute around the Cape of Good Hope, the Hormuz Strait has no viable maritime alternative.
霍尔木兹海峡对商船实质关闭已满两个月。Kpler于5月15日发布的集装箱情报数据揭示了危机规模：隶属全球主要班轮公司的53艘集装箱船被困波斯湾。截至WK20，其中42艘（79%）仍无法驶出。这不是临时拥堵，而是实质性运力冻结。与红海绕行危机不同——当时船只还可绕行好望角——霍尔木兹海峡并不存在可行的海上替代路线。

Effective 10 May 2026, MSC (world's largest container carrier) launched a new hybrid service bypassing the Strait of Hormuz. The route combines European ocean legs with overland truck transport across Saudi Arabia, connecting to feeder vessels in the Persian Gulf to serve UAE, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain and Saudi Arabian East Coast ports. This is a direct response to war-risk insurance suspensions that prevent standard Hormuz transits. MSC announced the service on 3 May, with operations beginning 10 May.

Hormuz Crisis: Structural Shipping Network Disruption — No Viable Sea Alternative
霍尔木兹危机：航运网络结构性中断 — 无可行海上替代路线
Geopolitics Rerouting Middle East
According to a Sina Finance analysis (12 May 2026), shipping companies including Maersk, MSC and Hapag-Lloyd face a fundamentally different challenge than the Red Sea crisis. Unlike Red Sea diversions — where vessels could route around the Cape of Good Hope adding ~10 days transit — the Hormuz Strait has NO viable maritime alternative. Any vessel needing to reach Gulf ports (UAE, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain, Saudi East Coast) must transit the Strait. War-risk insurers have cancelled or severely restricted coverage for Hormuz transits, making most commercial sailings impossible. Companies are rerouting to alternative discharge hubs (Jebel Ali, Salalah, Aqaba) and utilising inland transfer.
据财联社5月12日分析，马士基、MSC、赫伯罗特等船公司所面临的挑战与红海危机有本质不同。红海危机时船只还可绕行好望角（增加约10天航程），而霍尔木兹海峡没有任何可行的海上替代路线。任何需要抵达海湾港口（阿联酋、科威特、卡塔尔、巴林、沙特东海岸）的船舶必须过境霍尔木兹。战险保险公司已取消或严格限制霍尔木兹过境承保，使大多数商业航次不可能完成。船公司正改港至替代中转枢纽（杰贝阿里、萨拉拉、亚喀巴）并使用内陆转运。

Regulatory & Compliance — May 2026 New Rules
监管与合规 — 2026年5月新规
EU ICS2 Phase 3 Active Enforcement: Sea Freight Now Covered — H.S. Code Must Be 6-Digit
欧盟ICS2第三阶段主动执法：海运纳入覆盖范围 — HS编码须精确到6位
Compliance EU Regulatory
The EU Import Control System 2 (ICS2) Phase 3 has entered active enforcement as of May 2026, extending from air freight to sea freight and road cargo entering the EU. All Entry Summary Declarations (ENS) must now meet enhanced data quality standards: H.S. codes must be precise to the 6-digit level. EU customs will run automatic validation — incomplete data or cargo description mismatches trigger automatic rejection. There is NO tolerance window; the Q1 2026 grace period has ended. Chemicals, batteries, and temperature-controlled cargo are priority screening categories. Rejection at ENS filing prevents loading at Chinese ports.
欧盟进口控制系统2（ICS2）第三阶段已于2026年5月进入主动执法阶段，覆盖范围从空运扩展至海运及公路进境欧盟货物。所有入境摘要申报（ENS）须满足更高数据质量标准：HS编码必须精确到6位。欧盟海关将自动校验数据，数据不全或货描不匹配即自动驳回，无容错空间，Q1过渡期已结束。化工品、电池及温控货物为优先风险筛查品类。ENS申报失败将导致货物无法在中国港口装船。
CUL Ops Note (AI): CUL services loading EU-bound cargo from China must verify shippers' ENS compliance. Rejected ENS at origin = missed sailing. Advise shipper partners to update HS code databases per EU 2026 Combined Nomenclature and confirm freight forwarder is cross-checking against product safety data sheets. (AI解读，仅供参考)
CUL运营提示（AI解读，仅供参考）：CUL承载欧盟目的地货物的航线，须核实托运人的ENS合规情况。起运地ENS被拒=错过班期。建议提醒托运人合作伙伴按2026版欧盟联合分类目录更新HS编码数据库，并确认货代依据产品安全数据单（而非仅商业发票）核对HS编码。

A Xinhua / ChinaGate report (14 May 2026) highlights accelerating smart shipping development in China. Vessels can now plot routes autonomously, identify obstacles in real time, and dock at ports with increasing automation.
