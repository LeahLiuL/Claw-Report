📊 Container Freight Rates Surge — SCFI at 3,122, WCI Hits 18-Month High at $3,969
📊 集装箱运价飙升——SCFI达3,122点，WCI创18个月新高$3,969
⚡ CUL Direct Finance Commercial Global
Drewry's World Container Index (WCI) surged 12% WoW to $3,969/40ft as of June 18, marking an 18-month high. The Shanghai Containerized Freight Index (SCFI) stood at 3,121.69 on June 19, up 66.9% YoY. The rally is driven by early peak season demand, FIFA World Cup-related cargo rush, and ongoing Red Sea / Strait of Hormuz disruptions forcing longer transit routes.
Drewry世界集装箱指数（WCI）截至6月18日周环比飙升12%至$3,969/40尺，创18个月新高。上海出口集装箱运价指数（SCFI）6月19日报3,121.69点，同比上涨66.9%。此轮上涨由旺季提前、世界杯相关货物抢运以及红海/霍尔木兹持续中断导致航线绕行共同推动。
🎯 CUL Impact: Sustained high freight rates benefit all carriers, including CUL's regional and Middle East services. However, rising slot costs on partner-operated mainline services could pressure CUL's margins. Monitor Asia–Middle East rate trends closely given CUL's heavy exposure to this corridor. 运价持续高位利好所有船公司，包括CUL的区域和中东航线。但合作方主干航线舱位成本上升可能压缩CUL利润空间。鉴于CUL在中东走廊的较高敞口，需密切关注亚洲-中东运价走势。

⭐ Strait of Hormuz & Red Sea — Ongoing Disruptions Reshape Global Networks
⭐ 霍尔木兹海峡与红海——持续中断重塑全球航运网络
⭐ Featured ⚡ CUL Direct Ops Network Legal Middle East / Global
The Strait of Hormuz remains partially restricted with selective vessel passage continuing. Combined with ongoing Red Sea disruptions, carriers are maintaining Cape of Good Hope routings for Asia–Europe services, adding 10–14 days transit time and significant fuel costs. Insurance premiums for Gulf transits remain elevated. Some carriers (MSC, CMA CGM) have begun selectively testing Hormuz passages under specific conditions.
霍尔木兹海峡仍处于部分限制状态，选择性船舶通行继续。叠加红海持续中断，船公司维持好望角绕行方案运营亚欧航线，增加10-14天航程及大量燃油成本。海湾过境保险费率居高不下。部分船公司（MSC、CMA CGM）已开始在特定条件下选择性测试霍尔木兹通行。
🎯 CUL Impact: CUL Middle East services (Jebel Ali, Dammam, Sohar, Salalah) continue to face routing uncertainty. Monitor partner carrier (OOCL, Evergreen) decisions on Gulf service resumption. Cape detour premium remains $300–500K extra fuel per Middle East voyage. The Suez Canal weekly transit data shows gradual recovery in convoy volumes. CUL中东航线（杰贝阿里、达曼、苏哈尔、萨拉莱）持续面临路由不确定性。关注合作方（东方海外、长荣）关于恢复海湾服务的决策。好望角绕行每趟中东航次额外燃油成本$30-50万。苏伊士运河每周过境数据显示船队数量逐步恢复。

Early Peak Season Drives Record June Volumes — FIFA World Cup Effect
旺季提前来临推高6月货量——世界杯效应显现
Commercial Network Asia / Global
Container shipping is experiencing an unusually early peak season in June 2026, driven by: (1) FIFA World Cup 2026 (Jun 11–Jul 19) generating massive consumer goods and equipment shipments; (2) front-loading ahead of potential new US tariff measures; and (3) inventory restocking after prolonged supply chain disruptions. Asia–Europe and Transpacific routes are seeing the strongest volume growth.
2026年6月集装箱航运出现异常早的旺季，驱动因素包括：(1) 2026年FIFA世界杯（6月11日–7月19日）产生大量消费品和器材运输；(2) 潜在美国新关税措施前的抢运；(3) 长期供应链中断后的库存补货。亚欧和跨太平洋航线货量增长最为强劲。
